Meet the Visionaries

Crafting Memories:
Varun & Divya Dev

Co-Founders & Creative Directors

VD CREATION was born in 2021 from a simple realization: in an increasingly digital world, physical memories are becoming more precious than ever. Co-founders Varun (a structural framing designer) and Divya (a fine art print specialist) set out to merge digital printing technology with premium materials.

"We didn't want to make standard plastic photo frames that yellow and peel. We wanted to make frames that feel like sculptures, items you are proud to hang in your home or gift to people who matter. Every diamond-polished acrylic and handcrafted wooden casing goes through our personal validation before it is shipped."

"A frame shouldn't just border a photo; it should amplify the emotion that was captured."

How it is made

The Science of Crafting Memories

We combine state-of-the-art print engineering with careful carpentry to design long-lasting products.

Diamond-Polished Acrylics

We use 3mm and 5mm thick optical-grade plexiglass which features superior transparency to standard glass. All edges are polished using a diamond tip for a sleek crystal glow.

Copper LED Backlighting

Our glowing frames features high-density micro-LED strips soldered onto copper substrates. It emits a soft, flickering-free warm glow (3000K) that maximizes image contrast without glare.

Zink Mate Coatings

Our matte-coated frames use Zink-infused polymer coatings that resist finger smudges, moisture, and dust. Ideal for high humidity environments and long exposures.
