Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries regarding customization, upload sizes, shipping, and packaging.

For best results, we recommend uploading photos captured in good lighting with at least 150-300 DPI resolution. Standard mobile camera shots (above 4MB) work excellently. Our expert design editors also manually check every photo for print readiness.
Our LED frames are equipped with premium glowing warm yellow or cool white LED light tracks lined inside the wooden support casing. It comes with a 1.5-meter USB cable which can be connected to any phone charger adapter, power bank, or laptop.
We pack all frames inside multilayer high-density bubble sheets, custom thermocol molds, and high-strength 5-ply cartons. However, in the rare event of transit damage, we offer a **100% Free Replacement**. Just share a short unboxing video with our support via WhatsApp within 24 hours of delivery.
Since all items are custom-printed and handmade, production takes 1-2 business days. Shipping usually takes 3-5 business days depending on your location. Express delivery is available for metropolitan areas.
